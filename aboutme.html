I'am an aspiring web/software developer based out of San Diego, CA. I was drawn to the development world years ago in highschool when I teamed up with a friend to make a "Pong" game using JAVA, at that moment I was hooked. As soon as I could I signed up to take C++ and JAVA the following year. Upon successful completion I later on was introduced to algorithm scripting while in college at Embry-Riddle. That was challenging, but I love solving problems and then to see the fruitation of my hard work as a finished product brings me joy! I got married less than a year after finishing my first year in college and stepped away from coding for about 2 years.


Wanting a change in careers, I was talking to a friend about him using WordPress to create websites for his clients. After doing a little research and experimentation, the flame for development was re-ignited! I spent the next year and a half teaching myself HTML, CSS, Javascript, and most recently PHP. I completed severalCodecademycourses and enrolled in FreeCodeCamp.comcourses for Front-End & Full-Stack development. I am a fast learner and have a passion for development. My lifetime goal is to use my development skills to make a differece in the world.
