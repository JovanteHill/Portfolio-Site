Description:

This was a personal project I did for a friend who has a photography business. I had no requirements and he just let me do what I felt was right. I used a carousel to cylce through 4 of what I thought were his best conservative pictures that would capture a potential clients eye. Below his logo, navigaion links were used to cycle thru to the different pages. On the gallery page I used Javascript to create a light-box and a zoom effect to display the thumbnail in full resolution when an onclick event was triggered. While in the light-box mode you can click on the next image in the row and cycle that way. Included in the footer was round icons that link to his social media pages.

Made With:

HTML, CSS, Javascript and Bootstrap.
